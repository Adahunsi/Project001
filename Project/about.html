About Sesijolly Chemicals

Sesichem is a well-established branch of Sesijolly Enterprises, a leading name in the manufacturing industry. Founded in 2009 and based in Badagry, Lagos, Sesichem is a trusted provider of high-quality cleaning and disinfecting products. From the very beginning, Sesichem has been committed to delivering top-notch products that meet the highest standards of quality and efficacy. This dedication to excellence has made Sesichem a household name in Nigeria and beyond.

Development

Sesichem's product range includes a variety of cleaning and disinfecting solutions, such as SesiSuds, Germfreez, and EcoPestx. SesiSuds is a liquid soap that provides effective and gentle cleansing for all skin types. Germfreez is a powerful disinfectant that eliminates germs and bacteria, leaving surfaces clean and safe. EcoPestx is a potent pesticide that effectively controls pests and insects, keeping your home or business free from infestations. Sesichem's products have gained a reputation for their authenticity and effectiveness, making them a go-to choice for many consumers across Nigeria.
